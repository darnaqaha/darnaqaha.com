Services

We provide over the clock professional care in a friendly and relaxing environment. Dar Naqaha is not a nursing home for the elderly. We provide your loved ones with the best possible care. We accept patients of all ages, except children. We offer weekly full accommodation which includes medical care, nursing, daily physiotherapy, healthy meals and snacks, home laundry and free internet, at an affordable price (check “Service and Policy”). Our objective is to offer the best possible service and at the same time make it available and affordable to most families. In-patient rehab and recovery.

Every patient gets a special customized care. Our weekly packages include the following services:

  • Medical and Nursing service over the clock.
  • 3 meals and 2 snacks (compliance with medical plan and special dietary needs).
  • Daily physiotherapy.
  • Weekly psychotherapy (if needed).
  • Home laundry
  • Free Wi-Fi

Day Service Care

  • Chemotherapy
  • Immunotherapy
  • Blood transfusion and its derivatives
  • Any parental treatment based on prescription.
  • Bed sores care.
  • Medical examination (internal diseases, sugar and nerves) and the work up of tests for diagnosis and identification of treatment necessary
  • Preparation for surgical operations

Policy

To be able to provide the best possible care we are very strict about our policy:

  • Visiting hours between 12pm and 8pm
  • Bringing food to patients is prohibited.
  • Tipping the nursing and housekeeping staff is strictly prohibited; or asking for their personal numbers; leaving any cash with the patient
  • A first degree family member is necessary for admission with national ID for both.
  • Tax receipts are routinely provided, and all payments are in advance every 2 weeks.
  • Privacy policy is in place as no photos of the patients are put on our page or site for any purposes
